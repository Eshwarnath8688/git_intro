BRAHMANANDAM (MEMES OF GOD)

Kanneganti Bornnneganti Brahmanandam (born 1 February 1956) is an Indian actor and comedian known for his work in Telugu cinema.1 Recognised as one of India's finest and highest-paid comic actors,2,3 he holds the Guinness World Record for the most screen credits for a living actor, having appeared in over 1,000 films.4,5 Brahmanandam has received numerous accolades, including six Nandi Awards and two Filmfare Awards, and was honoured with the Padma Shri, India's fourth-highest civilian award, in 2009.6 He was also awarded an honorary doctorate by Acharya Nagarjuna University.7
